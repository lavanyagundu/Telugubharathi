Our Mission

Telugu Bharati promotes proliferation of language, literary and cultural traditions of Telugu speaking people, primarily amongst children and youth. Telugu language program in 6 cities (Bellevue, Bothell, Kent, Redmond, Sammamish, Issaquah) in Washington State, USA for children (5 years and up) during the academic calendar year.
